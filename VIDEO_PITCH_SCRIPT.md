## Key Points to Remember:

### Speak From Heart:
- Use real examples (Dilbar Ali, 8 years old, Korangi)
- Talk about mothers, children, families
- Make it personal - "hum bhi in galiyon mein paley hain"

### Keep It Simple:
- No technical words
- "System check karta hai" instead of "AI verification"
- "Photo dalni padti hai" instead of "image upload required"

### Show, Don't Just Tell:
- Screen recordings of actual app
- Point to features while explaining
- Let them see it working

### Create Urgency:
- "27 log mare"
- "Har din jo guzarta hai..."
- "Agli saal zero ho sakta hai"

## Practice Tips:

1. **Rehearse the emotional parts** - Don't rush "27 log mare"
2. **Pause after heavy statements** - Let it sink in
3. **Make eye contact with camera** - You're talking to decision makers
4. **Believe in what you're saying** - You built this to help people
5. **End strong** - "Pakistan Zindabad" with conviction
